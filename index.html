Our Story

Vidhitsa is a Sanskrit term meaning an intention to design or to do.

Vidhitsa Design Studio is a leading interior design studio based in Gurugram, India. Started in 2009, our experienced team of professionals has planned more than 1 million square feet of luxury residential, commercial and retail spaces. We are dedicated to providing our clients with premium interior design services that bring their vision to life.

Our team works in close collaboration with our clients to ensure their needs and desires are met. With a keen eye for detail and a passion for design, we strive to bring our clients the highest quality work and results.
